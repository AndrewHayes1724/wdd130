Content

Home page

Different Ideas and Possibilities

People will come to my site so that they can learn what is possible in the creation of car designs within the game of rocket league. There are 1000's of decals, an RGB color palette, 1000's of wheels and other decorations for your car which make the design possibilities endless. Ever wonder how to sort all the decals from rarest to least rare? On our site we will show you exactly how to do it so that you can find the coolest decals for your car. The type of things you will learn and questions you will have answered on this site are first, where to start with designing your car. Then after that, what is possible rather than just simple car designs, you can do anything you want just about. The type of activity that visitors will want to complete on this site is to get ideas on the types of cars they want to make, and then exactly how to put that into action within the game.
