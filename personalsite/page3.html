Customization

Once in game, hit F2 to open up the Bakkes Mod console

from there, go to the "Items" tab, and enable item mods.

The fun starts here

from here you have free reign over the thousands of different decals and other available customizable options on your car along with changing the colors of the primary and secondary aspects to the decal

Submit your cars here for a chance to get featured on our page!
